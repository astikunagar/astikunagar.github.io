How It Works

From Inquiry to Your Doorstep

Every bulk order follows the same transparent pipeline — so you always know where your shipment stands.

01

Share Your Requirement

Tell us the products, quantities (in litres or 5L cans), delivery location, and preferred timeline. Our team responds with a detailed quote within 1–2 business days.

02

Order Confirmation & Scheduling

Once you confirm the purchase order and advance payment terms, we slot your batch into our production schedule and share an estimated dispatch date.

03

Dedicated Batch Production

Your order is blended at our Mumbai facility using the same retail-grade formulas. Large runs are produced in dedicated batches — not repacked from leftover stock.

04

Quality Assurance & Labelling

Each batch passes pH, viscosity, and efficacy checks. Cans are labelled, sealed, and batch-coded before they leave the filling line.

05

Palletizing, Dispatch & Delivery

Cans are pallet-wrapped for transit. We coordinate with our logistics partners for PAN-India dispatch and share tracking details once the shipment is on the road.

  • Transit time is additional — allow 2–7 days depending on destination city and carrier availability.

Manufacturing

What Happens on the Production Floor

Bulk does not mean bulk-grade. Every litre we produce for commercial clients uses the identical formulation that goes into our 1 litre retail bottles — same surfactants, same fragrance profile, same quality bar.

Batch Blending

Liquids are mixed in stainless-steel vessels sized for your order volume, then filled into 5L HDPE cans on automated lines.

Batch Testing

Random samples from each batch are tested for pH, appearance, and cleaning performance before release.

Traceability

Every can carries a batch code and manufacturing date. Certificate of Analysis available on request for institutional buyers.

Dispatch

Packaging & Delivery
